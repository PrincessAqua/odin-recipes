Ingredients

  • Rice: This creamy rice pudding recipe starts with white rice cooked until tender.
  • Milk: You'll need two cups of milk, divided.
  • Sugar: The rice pudding is sweetened with white sugar.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the flavor of other ingredients, but it won't make the pudding taste salty.
  • Egg: An egg adds moisture and helps blind the pudding together.
  • Raisins: Raisins lend burst of fruity flavor.
  • Butter: Butter Adds even more moisture and richness.
  • Vanilla: A dash of vanilla enhances the overall flavor of the rice pudding.

How do you make rice pudding?

Here's a brief overview of what you cant expect when you make homemade rice pudding:

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Cook the coocked rice, some of the milk, sugar, and salt over medium heat until thick.
  3. Stir in the remaining milk, egg, and raisins. Cook for two more minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the butter and vainilla.
